## 06. Guard Your Energy - peace

*Guard Your Energy*

Your energy is finite and everything wants a piece of it — protect it like something valuable, because it is. Say no to drains, yes to what fuels you, and stop giving your best hours to your worst habits.


## 13. Protect Your Peace - peace

*Protect Your Peace*

Peace isn't passive — it's something you defend with boundaries, choices, and the willingness to walk away from chaos. A calm mind is a competitive advantage; guard it accordingly.


## 36. Get Comfortable With No - peace

*Get Comfortable With No*

Every yes to the wrong thing is a no to the right one — get comfortable declining what doesn't serve your direction. No is a complete sentence and a powerful tool.

## N24. Leave The Argument - peace

*Leave The Argument*

The fight will still be there tomorrow — step away before you say the thing you'll replay all night. Walk away and your nerves get a reset scrolling never gives.

## N25. Close The Day On Purpose - peace

*Close The Day On Purpose*

Work bleeds into sleep when you don't draw a line — shut the laptop and be done. End the day on purpose and your body finally believes it's allowed to stop.

## N26. Stop Feeding The Noise - peace

*Stop Feeding The Noise*

Your phone isn't rest — it's a firehose of other people's urgency. Put it down for twenty minutes and remember what quiet sounds like.

## N27. Breathe Before Reply - peace

*Breathe Before Reply*

Reacting in the heat costs more than the moment — breathe before you reply. Ten breaths and your response comes from choice, not adrenaline.

## N28. Protect The Off Switch - peace

*Protect The Off Switch*

You can't recover while you're still performing — protect the off switch. Flip it off and tomorrow's energy arrives full.

## N29. Walk Away From Chaos - peace

*Walk Away From Chaos*

Chaos takes every minute you hand it — walk away before it drains you dry. Leave on time and peace isn't vacation-only.

## N30. Let Silence Work - peace

*Let Silence Work*

Constant input keeps the mind agitated — let ten minutes in silence work. Sit with quiet and hear what actually needs you.

## N31. Hold The Boundary - peace

*Hold The Boundary*

People test every vague boundary — hold the line calmly and let no be complete. Hold it without apology and energy stops leaking.

## N32. Unclench Your Jaw - peace

*Unclench Your Jaw*

Stress hides in the body first — unclench your jaw, drop your shoulders, exhale long. Release it and your mind gets an inch of calm.

## N33. Choose Less Input - peace

*Choose Less Input*

More information rarely brings more peace — choose less input today. Cut the feed and overwhelm loses its fuel.
