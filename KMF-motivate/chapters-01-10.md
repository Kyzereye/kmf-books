# Chapters 1–10

## 01. Get on Your Deathbed

*Act Before It's Over*

Imagine you're on your deathbed looking back at your life — what would you wish you had done, said, or become? Let that clarity strip away everything that doesn't matter and push you to act on what does, starting today.

## 02. Stay Hungry

*Never Get Full*

Never let yourself get full — keep a fire in your belly for something bigger than where you are now. Hunger is the fuel that turns ordinary effort into extraordinary results.

## 03. Tell Yourself a True Lie

*Believe The Better You*

Picture the best version of yourself as if it's already real, even when it feels like fiction. Act from that vision and your mind will start catching up to the person you're pretending to be.

## 04. Keep Your Eyes on the Prize

*Block Out The Noise*

Block out the noise, the fear, and the distractions — lock your focus on what you're actually going after. A team that forgets the scoreboard loses the game; don't take your eyes off yours.

## 05. Learn to Sweat in Peace

*Train Hard Stay Calm*

Train harder in the calm so the storm feels easy. The more you practice when nothing's on the line, the more unstoppable you become when everything is.

## 06. Simplify Your Life

*Cut What Doesn't Matter*

Cut the clutter — in your schedule, your goals, and your head — until only what matters remains. Simplicity isn't laziness; it's the sharpest weapon you have against overwhelm.

## 07. Look for the Lost Gold

*Find Gold Everywhere*

What you see in the world is a mirror of what's inside you — look for the opportunity, the lesson, the gift in every situation. The gold is always there; you just have to decide to find it.

## 08. Push All Your Own Buttons

*Take Control Of You*

Stop waiting for something outside you to flip the switch — learn what fires you up and hit those buttons yourself. You are the pilot; take the controls.

## 09. Build a Track Record

*Stack Small Wins*

Prove it to yourself one small win at a time — write it down, stack it up, and let the evidence speak. Confidence isn't something you feel first; it's something you build and grow by showing up.

## 10. Welcome the Unexpected

*Open The Curveball*

Creativity isn't always invention — sometimes it's just being open when life throws you a curveball. The unexpected isn't your enemy; it's often the door you didn't know to look for.
