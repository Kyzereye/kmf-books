# Chapters 91–100

## 91. Exploit Your Weakness

*Use The Weak Angle*

Your weakness isn't always something to hide — sometimes it's the angle nobody else is playing. Know it, own it, and use it strategically instead of letting it use you.

## 92. Try Becoming the Problem

*Become The Problem*

You can't solve what you don't fully understand — step inside the problem, see it from the inside out, and let that perspective reveal the solution everyone else is missing. Become the problem and you'll know exactly how to beat it.

## 93. Enlarge Your Objective

*Make The Goal Bigger*

If your goal doesn't make you a little nervous, it's too small — enlarge it until it demands a bigger version of you to achieve it. Think bigger and your daily actions will rise to match.

## 94. Give Yourself Flying Lessons

*Learn To Fly Higher*

You weren't born knowing how to do this — learn the skill, take the lesson, stretch into territory that feels unfamiliar. Every new capability is a flying lesson that takes you somewhere you couldn't reach before.

## 95. Hold Your Vision Accountable

*Vision Must Match Action*

A vision without accountability is just a poster on the wall — check your daily actions against where you say you're going and close the gap. If what you're doing today doesn't serve the vision, the vision is just decoration.

## 96. Build Your Power Base

*Stack Your Foundations*

Champions aren't built on one lucky break — they're built on fundamentals: habits, skills, relationships, and health stacked over time. Build the base and the heights take care of themselves.

## 97. Connect Truth to Beauty

*Truth Needs Beauty Too*

The most powerful motivation comes when what is true and what is beautiful point in the same direction — find the elegance in your purpose and let it inspire you. Truth without beauty feels like duty; together they feel like destiny.

## 98. Read Yourself a Story

*Write Your Own Story*

Write the story of your life the way you'd want to read it — not fantasy, but the narrative of who you're becoming and where you're headed. You are both the author and the main character; make it worth turning the page.

## 99. Laugh for No Reason

*Laugh On Purpose*

Laughter rewires your chemistry in seconds — do it on purpose, even when nothing's funny, and feel the shift in your energy. Joy isn't a luxury for when you've earned it; it's a tool you can use right now.

## 100. Walk with Love and Death

*Love Hard Clock Running*

Carry love in one hand and the reality of mortality in the other — let love guide what you do and death remind you that the clock is running. Walk with both and you'll never waste another day on what doesn't matter.
