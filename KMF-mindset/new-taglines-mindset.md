## 03. Earn Your Confidence - mindset

*Earn Your Confidence*

Confidence isn't something you talk yourself into — it's the receipt you get after showing up every day. Stack enough proof and belief stops being a question.

## 29. Cut The Excuse - mindset

*Cut The Excuse*

The moment you catch yourself negotiating with your own weakness, cut it — no rebuttal, no second hearing, just done. Excuses survive on attention; starve them.

## 31. Demand More From You - mindset

*Demand More From You*

Nobody else is going to push you as hard as you can push yourself — demand a higher standard from the one person who's always watching. You already know what you're capable of when you stop settling.

## 33. Drop The Story - mindset

*Drop The Story*

The narrative you keep telling about why you can't is the real obstacle — drop the story and you're left with just the situation, and situations are solvable. You're not your backstory; you're what you do next.

## 41. Keep Your Standards High - mindset

*Keep Your Standards High*

Lower your standards and life meets you there — keep them high and you keep reaching. What you tolerate is what you get.

## 47. Outwork Your Doubts - mindset

*Outwork Your Doubts*

Doubt doesn't disappear when you think harder about it. It disappears when you work harder than the doubt can keep up with you. Put your energy into the work, and that effort is what quiets the noise in your head.

## N17. Reframe The Setback - mindset

*Reframe The Setback*

A setback isn't a verdict — it's data with emotional packaging. Reframe it as information and your next move gets clearer.

## N18. Trade Victim For Agent - mindset

*Trade Victim For Agent*

Victim language hands away power — trade it for empowered thinking. Ask what you can influence and options return.

## N19. Replace Can't With How - mindset

*Replace Can't With How*

"I can't" ends the search — replace it with "how could I?" and let your brain work the problem.

## N20. See The Lesson First - mindset

*See The Lesson First*

Failure stings less when it teaches — find the lesson before you find someone to blame. The loss buys something for the next round.

## N21. Stop Fortune Telling - mindset

*Stop Fortune Telling*

Your mind predicts catastrophe the evidence doesn't support — stop fortune telling. Stay with facts and fear shrinks to workable size.

## N22. Name The Story - mindset

*Name The Story*

The story you tell is shaping the outcome — name it and decide if it's true. Change the story and you stop acting from fiction.

## N23. Assume You Can Learn - mindset

*Assume You Can Learn*

"I'm not that kind of person" ages badly — assume you can learn and let evidence update the label.
